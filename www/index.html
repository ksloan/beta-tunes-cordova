About

This app was developed by Kevin Sloan, Toronto 2013. Please share this app with your brothers and keep the singing fraternity's songs in your pocket at all times!

Song lyrics were taken from the 2005 revision of the Beta Tunes Songbook. Special Thanks to the Alpha Chapter for hosting the MP3s.

If you have feedback, comments, or revisions please contact me through my website or by email.


NOTE: Listening to songs streams data over your wifi or cellular network.
